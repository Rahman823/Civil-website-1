You May Ask

What is SAZ Infratech?
It's an initiative, started by Md Sami ur Rahman and Md Seraj Ansari, Which renders services like Road Surveys, Bridge Design, Building Construction and Architect Design etc.
Why to Trust SAZ?
Well, we've been in this business since 25 years, you can check our profile or reviews of our clients.
Will it cost too much?
It'll cost just appropriate amount for any service, besides we also render our several discounts to our certain clients, and we provide free fixes as well for certain time period.
How long will it take to start?
We generally start work just after all the documentation work is done but if some clients requires things to be done hastely, that can be managed as well.
Why merely SAZ?
SAZ infratech won't just render the best of all work but the best of all charge as well, which has been satisfying our clients for 25 years.
How come SAZ render so many services?
At first, SAZ Infratech was only for Road Survey, Bridge design and Road design. But later on when our company grew into a much bigger company then we started adding other services like Building construction and House renovation etc.
Is SAZ good with it's new services?
We are just as good with our new services as we are with our earlier ones, we have an extraordinary team of engineers and workers who can render the best of all work.
Can SAZ take extreme workload?
We've finished works under given time, for such big projects like Ganga bridge and we've renovated mansions like Windsor Castle for the Royals.
What if the work is not finished under the given time?
It is not likely to happen but if it happened then we provide a full money back.
Will the work be good in less time?
We'll make sure best of the works is provided, to get the work in least time, we generally recruit more engineers and workers, which may cost extra.
